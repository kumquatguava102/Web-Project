Resale Glossary

Grail
A highly sought-after, extremely rare item that defines a collector's journey.
Legit Check (LC)
The thorough process of authenticating a designer garment through physical inspection.
Deadstock (DS)
An item that is brand new, never worn, and often includes its original packaging or tags.
